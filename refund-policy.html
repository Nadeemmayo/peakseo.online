Service Policy

Refund Policy

At PeakSEO, we aim to provide reliable guest posting, link building and SEO services with clear delivery, replacement and refund terms.

Last Updated: 2026

This Refund Policy explains how refunds and replacements are handled for PeakSEO services, including guest posting, link building, niche edits, authority backlinks and other off-page SEO services.

Important Note: We always try to resolve service issues first through replacement, correction or publisher follow-up before processing any refund.

Service Satisfaction

PeakSEO strives to provide high-quality SEO services and reliable backlink placements. If an approved guest post link is not published, removed within the guaranteed period or delivered incorrectly, we will review the issue and provide a suitable solution.

Replacement Policy

If a guest post or backlink is removed within the guaranteed period, we may provide a replacement link on the same website or a similar quality website, depending on publisher availability and placement conditions.

  • Replacement may be offered if the link is removed unexpectedly.
  • Replacement may be offered if the delivered website is different from the approved order.
  • Replacement may be offered if there is a publisher-side issue after placement.

Refund Eligibility

A refund may be considered if the approved service cannot be delivered and no suitable replacement is available. Refunds are reviewed case by case based on service status, publisher response and delivery progress.

Non-Refundable Conditions

Refunds are generally not applicable in the following cases:

  • The guest post or backlink has already been published and approved.
  • The link has been indexed or processed by search engines.
  • The client changes requirements after work has started.
  • The publisher rejects changes that were not part of the original order.
  • Ranking results are not guaranteed, because SEO performance depends on many external factors.

SEO Result Disclaimer

PeakSEO provides SEO services to improve authority, visibility and backlink strength. However, we do not guarantee exact ranking positions, traffic numbers or fixed Google results because search engine algorithms are controlled by third parties.

Order Cancellation

Orders can only be cancelled before work has started. Once content writing, outreach, publisher communication or link placement work has started, cancellation may not be possible.

Processing Time

Approved refunds, where applicable, may take a reasonable processing time depending on payment method and internal verification.

How To Request Support

If you face any issue with your guest post, backlink, niche edit or SEO service, please contact us with your order details, live URL, target URL and issue description. Our team will review the case and respond with the best available solution.
